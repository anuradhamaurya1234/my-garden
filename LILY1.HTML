FLOWER OF LILY




Lilies are a genus of flowering plants that grow from bulbs12. They are usually quite tall and are perennials1. Lilies have six plain or strikingly marked tepals (“petals”) and are often trumpet-shaped, sitting atop a tall, erect stem with narrow, long, lance-shaped leaves3. The flowers consist of six petal-like segments, which may form the shape of a trumpet, with a more or less elongated tube4. Lilies come in many beautiful colors, including pink, gold, red, orange, and white.
